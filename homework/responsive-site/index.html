TRI DELTA
UNIVERSITY OF MIAMI

Tri Delta is one of six Panhellenic sororities on the University of Miami's campus. Created in 1888, Tri Delta was founded on the principle of “being kind alike to all”. Our sorority prides itself on women that live, learn, and lead in a kind fashion. Tri Delta’s colors are silver, gold, and blue and our main symbols are the pearl, the pine, the pansy, and the dolphin. In 1999, our organization partnered with St. Jude Children’s Research Hospital, and since then we have raised over $20 million for the kids being treated there.
