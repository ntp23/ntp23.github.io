How to Join

Because we want everyone joining Greek Life to find the sorority where they feel the most at home, Panhellenic at the University of Miami does not allow anyone to rush a specific sorority.

All girls interested in joining must register for recruitment on Panhellenic's website and go through the formal process of meeting girls in all six sororities before choosing the one they want.

To Register go to
https://enroll.icsrecruiter.com/pan/MIAUNV#/enroll/enrollment-disclaimer
Recruitment Schedule

January 8th - 9th: Open House

During Open House you will talk to girls in all six chapters to get a general feel for each sorority. Each party is 25 minutes long.

January 10th: Philanthropy

On this day of recruitment, you will learn more about each chapters philanthropy and the events each sorority hosts to raise money for it. Each party is 30 minutes long.

January 11th: Invitational

During Invitation you will visit up to 3 chapters and will learn more about the sorority's sisterhood. This is one of the most important days of recruitment and after all of your parties you will choose your Top 2.

January 12th: Preference

Today is the last day of Formal Recruitment and you will only be visiting 2 chapters. Each party is 50 minutes long and will show you an example of a ritual that members perform.

January 13th: Bid Day

(aka the best day of your life) is when you find out which chapter you will soon become a part of. On Bid Day you will meet with your Recruitment Counselor to get an envelope with your bid. At noon, everyone will open their bid cards and get a shirt to put on to reveal which sorority you will now be joining. After the big reveal, you will spend the day with your new sisters and celebrate your entrance into Greek Life.
