Involved Deltas

Our chapter is extremely involved on campus and hosts numerous events throughout the year to raise money for St. Jude Children's Research Hospital. These include Triple Play Softball Tournament, Tees fore Tots Golf Tournament, Sincerely Yours, and the St. Jude Walk/Run. This year we raised over $100,000 for the kids and were the highest fundraising chapter in South Florida.


What is St. Jude's?

St. Jude Children's Research hospital is a pediatric treatment and research facility for children with cancer and other deadly diseases. Founded in 1962 by Danny Thomas, St. Jude was created to allow families to focus on their child's health without worrying about paying for treatment, hotel rooms, food, etc. It costs $1.8 million per day to keep the hospital running however patients and families will never receive a bill. No child is ever denied treatment and the hospital has a 94% survival rate. In honor of Tri Delta becoming the largest single fundraising committee in 2014, St. Jude built a short-term housing facility and named it Tri Delta Place.
